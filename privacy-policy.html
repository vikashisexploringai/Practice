# Privacy Policy **Last Updated:** August 11, 2025 This Privacy Policy explains how our Custom GPT (“the GPT”) interacts with data when you use it. ## Information Collection The GPT does **not** collect, store, or process any personal information about you. When you use this GPT, your queries are sent to: - **GitHub Raw Content URLs** under the repository [vikashisexploringai/Practice](https://github.com/vikashisexploringai/Practice). ## Information Usage The GPT only: - Reads public JSON files from the above GitHub repository. - Returns that data in response to your request. No personal identifiers, messages, or other private data are stored or shared outside of the conversation. ## Third-Party Services The GPT fetches data from [GitHub Raw Content](https://docs.github.com/en/rest) over HTTPS. GitHub may log requests to its servers as part of standard operations, but no special tracking is implemented. ## Changes to This Policy If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be posted in the repository and reflected in the GPT’s description. ## Contact For questions about this policy, please contact the repository owner via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/vikashisexploringai/Practice/issues).
